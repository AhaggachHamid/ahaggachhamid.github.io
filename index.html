About Me

I am a PhD student undertaking a CIFRE thesis in collaboration between Syartec—a leading software and application development company focused on solutions for car dealers—and the LIB laboratory at the University of Burgundy. My research is centered on the application of data science and artificial intelligence, including machine learning and deep learning techniques, within the automotive industry. I am an avid AI enthusiast, currently serving both as a data scientist and PhD candidate at Syartec. My journey in AI commenced with a Master's degree in Data Science, establishing a solid foundation in AI and its diverse applications. Over time, I have developed various AI-driven tools and applications, including web applications that incorporate Natural Language Processing.

My expertise includes the development and deployment of machine learning models. I am skilled in fine-tuning Large Language Models and employing Retrieval Augmented Generation techniques to improve the accuracy and reliability of generative AI models by integrating information from external sources.
